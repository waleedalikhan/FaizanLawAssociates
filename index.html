Experience when it matters most

Getting the best result for your case means having a team of attorneys with experience in the local courts of Lahore, Pakistan. It means having a team with proven results and the detailed knowledge to construct the strongest arguments on your behave, whether it’s so you can get compensated for an injury, need to protect your best interests in family or marital dispute, or need to convince a jury that you are innocent of crime.

Our attorneys at Faizan Law Associates have decades of combined experience practicing in Pasrur and Lahore City as well as a legal advisor of Paragon City and Eden City. We know the law and we know the courts. Our team includes a former prosecutor who can provide an advantageous perspective on your criminal or traffic defense, as well as your family issues. Our firm also can help if you are going through a Divorce, child custody or child support proceedings.
